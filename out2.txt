Selection-Sort(A)
1.  for i = 1 to A.length -1
2.      min = i
3.      for j = i+1 to A.length
4.          if A[j] < A[min]
5.              min = j
6.      swap A[min] and A[i]

Basic-Counting-Sort(A, k)
1.  Allocate array C[1..k]
2.  for i=1 to A.length
3.      C[A[i]] = C[A[i]]+1
4.  h = 1
5.  for i = 1 to k
6.      for j = 1 to C[i]
7.          A[h] = i
8.          h = h+1
